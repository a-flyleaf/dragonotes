In alphabetical order. Search using CTRL+F.

Most of these terms are informal; they’re rarely if ever used in official material, but appear frequently on the forums and in user profiles.

TermDescription

AH

Acronym for the Auction House.

related: coli, CR, FF, MP, PA

B/S/T

Buying/selling/trading. Often seen in thread titles & can be removed or swapped out as needed.

related: RAF

coli

Short form of the Coliseum.

related: AH, CR, elim, FF, MP

CR

Refers to the Crossroads and transactions faciliated by it.

related: AH, FF, MP

dom

Short for dominance. Often used in the context of "dom battles" or "dom raffles," user-run events to enliven the weekly competitions.

see also: Flight Rising FAQ > What is Exalting? What is Dominance?

related: fodder, IF, OOF

double

A dragon with two of the same color.

related: triple, XYZ

elim

Short for battle stone Eliminate. One of the site’s most useful items.

see also: Culex’s Coliseum guide.

related: coli

fam

Short for familiar.

see also: Flight Rising FAQ > What are Familiars? What can I do with them?

FF

Acronym for Festive Favors.

related: AH, coli, CR, MP

firstgen

A dragon without parents, either because it’s a progenitor or was hatched from an egg.

related: G1 & G2, progen

fodder

Dragons intended for exaltation. In the context of "baldwin fodder," items that have no better use than transmutation.

see also: Flight Rising FAQ > What is Exalting? What is Dominance?

FR

Acronym for Flight Rising.

G1 & G2

Denotes a dragon’s generation. Rarely used past the second generation.

related: firstgen

g/kT, g:kT

Shorthands for the two currency types, gems (G/g) and treasure (T; kT = 1,000). The ratio, denoted by the colon, can fluctuate.

For example, a dragon or item selling for "15g/kT" can be purchased for either 15 gems or 15,000 treasure. If a user is valuing 1 gem at 1000T, they could write it as 1g:1kT or 1g:1000T.

see also: Flight Rising FAQ > Treasure & Gems

hatchery

A user-run dragon sales shop, usually featuring two or more designated breeding pairs.

hibden

Short for hibernal den.

see also: Lair Revamp + Hibernal Den announcement.

IF

Acronym for in-flight, activities exclusive to members of a certain flight.

Could also just be "if" in all caps. Context is key.

related: dom, OOF

lore

Catch-all term for fictional development within the world of Sornieth. Can refer to official materials such as those in the Encyclopedia or users’ own writing.

lvl

Short for a dragon’s Coliseum level. Lvl25 maximum.

If you’re familiar with Pokémon or similar RPGs, this will look familiar.

see also: Culex’s Coliseum guide

MP

Acronym for the Marketplace.

related: AH, coli, CR, FF

OOF

Acronym for out-of-flight, activities open to mebmers of all flights.

Could also just be "oof" in all caps. Context is key.

related: dom, IF

PA

Acronym for Private Auction, an auction only visible by the seller and recipient.

related: AH, CR

progen(s)

Short for progenitor(s). Your first two dragons; "custom" is the one whose colors you choose upon joining, and the other is "random."

see also: Flight Rising FAQ > What is a ‘progenitor’?

RAF

Short for raffle. May also be marked with an [R] alone.

related: B/S/T

scry

Refers to the Predict Morphology feature, because no one wants to type "morphology" every time. As a verb (ex. "scrying and crying"), refers to the act of making a dragon on this page; as a noun (ex. "here are my dream dragon scries"), refers to the image generated.

skincent

Refers to a Custom Skin that covers over 30% but under 100% of a dragon. Can also be used as a catch-all for both skins and accents.

triple

A dragon with three of the same color.

related: double, XYZ

XYZ

Alongside XXX, XXY, XYX, and XYY, shorthand for dragon primary/secondary/tertiary color patterns; the letters indicate what colors match where.

For example, if your dragon’s colors are Black and Grey, XYX = Black/Grey/Black and XYY = Black/Grey/Grey.

related: double, triple
