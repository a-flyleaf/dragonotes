Range & Variants

Originating in the Southern Icefield, the first Frostbite dragons are thought to be a small cluster of Tundras based in the [eternal blizzard place]. Their unique coloration led to the development of a specialized ambush technique, which they used to capture Shade entities. Over time, the group's range expanded, initially by northward land travel and later with branch-offs into new territories. Many of these offshoot groups eventually broke contact with the [Icefield] base, resulting in the scattered population seen today.

Despite the wider territorial range, and by extension shifting loyalties from deity to deity, Frostbite dragons remain distinct in both [coloration](#physiology) and [culture](#clans). Their impact and spread throughout Sornieth is poorly understood; research into is ongoing.

What is this…?

Quick fourthwall-break. If you've somehow stumbled upon this page without knowing what it is, hi! Flight Rising is a browser-based dragon collection game, on which many people write little stories and develop worlds for their dragons. FR has an established setting, Sornieth, but players are free to pick and choose which aspects of the "canonical" lore they use (if any).
